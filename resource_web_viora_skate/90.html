1990

In the 1990's, skateboarding started focusing more on street skateboarding. Most boards are 7 1/4 to 8 in and 30-32 inches long with a largely symmetrical shape with a relatively narrow width.

The board hasn't changed much from the 90s til now, but the concave may be a little deeper. However, people are starting to ride their own custom shaped boards more and more!
